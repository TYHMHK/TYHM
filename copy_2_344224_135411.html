Brand introduction
Coffee Time was founded in 2008 and is welcomed by fashionistas and urban elites. In modern fast-paced life, coffee time hopes to provide people with a relaxed and leisurely "slow" space with its own characteristics. Coffee time is designed with the idea of comfort and freedom, advocating the unique nature, adding Turkish and Southeast Asian style lighting on the elements of nature, and matching chairs in different forms, so that customers can experience the new concept of coffee culture, and let everyone enjoy romance and leisure time. At the same time, it can satisfy customers'gradual improvement of taste and demand and enjoy the fragrance of lips and teeth.
During the day, the sun shines through the glass windows, and the Indian red curtains leave a shadow of the corridor, which is interesting against the wooden floor. People are bathed in the sunshine in the coffee shop, and the sound of the flute is clear and lively, and they are always intoxicated. At night, the lively and passionate cafes, Jazz, India and Latin sounds of nature meet, people exchange and understand each other, or continue the debate in the classroom, while the lover of fish in muddy water gently carries on their movements, reflecting interesting, it is a symphony. 
 Every day, the people coming and going in the cafe are more lovely scenery. 
 Sculpture time, always adhering to the professional quality of coffee management, good industry reputation so that this 11-year-old chain cafe in Beijing has been keeping vitality. In addition, the Western-style simple meals managed under the brand also become classic dishes with a variety of items and delicious tastes. It is our desire to constantly absorb the new elements of the cafe and gradually integrate LOHAS life concepts, such as movies, music, reading, fetishes, etc. into it, to discover life unconsciously, to love life and to provide more comfortable experience for life.
Coffee beans contain about 100 different substances, including caffeine, tannic acid, oil and nitrogen compounds, 44-100 mg of caffeine per 100 grams of instant coffee and 64-124 mg of caffeine per 100 grams of prepared coffee. Coffee is a stimulant that has many effects on the human body. It can diuretic, stimulate the central nervous system and respiratory system, expand blood vessels, speed up the heartbeat, enhance the strength of the striated muscles, and relieve brain and muscle fatigue.
